Drawing in the 3D space allows users to have creative freedom of viewing their creations from all angles. Having only a little knowledge of Three.js, I wanted to push and familiarize myself with this Javascript library. In just three days, through hard work and determination, I created this fun drawing application where users can draw on the moon. This application not only allows you to draw in the 3D space, but also uses a physics engine so you can explore gravity on the moon or other planets! Mental Mentality

During Tufts University’s annual 24 hour Polyhack event, three of my friends and I came together to build a website from the ground up. We wanted to focus on a topic that we were passionate about and develop a demo site that could be useful on our campus. With our demo site, we ended up coding for about 18 hours and won 1st place in the event. Check out our demo site!
